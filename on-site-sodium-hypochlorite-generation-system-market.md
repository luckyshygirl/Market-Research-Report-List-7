<p><p>The on-site sodium hypochlorite generation system market can be categorized into two main types: brine systems and seawater systems. Brine systems utilize sodium chloride mixed with water to produce sodium hypochlorite through electrolysis, offering cost-effectiveness and control over production. In contrast, seawater systems leverage abundant ocean water, using its natural salt content for hypochlorite generation. Both systems provide a sustainable solution for water treatment and disinfection, reducing transportation costs and safety risks associated with handling chlorine.</p></p>
