<p><p>Freight forwarder market types include Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than Container Load (LCL). FCL involves a single shipper using an entire container, ideal for larger shipments, providing cost efficiency and faster transit. LCL accommodates multiple shippers sharing container space, suitable for smaller shipments, though it may lead to longer transit times due to consolidation. Other markets can encompass various transportation methods, such as air freight and breakbulk shipping, addressing specific needs such as oversized cargo or urgent deliveries.</p></p>
