<p><p>The warehouse barcode scanner market comprises two main types: handheld and fixed scanners. Handheld warehouse barcode scanners offer portability and flexibility, allowing users to scan barcodes in various locations within the warehouse, enhancing mobility during inventory management. Fixed warehouse barcode scanners, on the other hand, are stationary devices installed at strategic points to automatically scan products as they move through defined areas, such as conveyor belts or loading docks, optimizing efficiency in tracking and processing inventory.</p></p>
