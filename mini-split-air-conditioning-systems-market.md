<p><p>Mini split air conditioning systems are classified into air cooling and water cooling markets. Air cooling systems utilize refrigerant to absorb heat from indoor air and release it outside, providing efficient temperature control in residential and commercial settings. In contrast, water cooling systems circulate chilled water through coils to cool air, making them suitable for larger buildings or industrial applications. Both systems offer flexibility in installation and energy efficiency, catering to diverse cooling needs in various environments.</p></p>
