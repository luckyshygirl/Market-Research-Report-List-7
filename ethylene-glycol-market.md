<p><p>The ethylene glycol market comprises three primary types: Monoethylene Glycol (MEG), Diethylene Glycol (DEG), and Triethylene Glycol (TEG). DEG acts mainly as a solvent, plasticizer, and antifreeze, while TEG is utilized primarily in gas dehydration and as a humectant in various applications. Each type has distinct properties and uses, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
