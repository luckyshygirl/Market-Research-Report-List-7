<p><strong>The Intraoral Dental X-ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Occlusal X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraoral dental X-ray systems market consists of three primary types: bitewing X-rays, periapical X-rays, and occlusal X-rays. Bitewing X-rays are used to view the upper and lower teeth in a single image, helping detect cavities between teeth. Periapical X-rays capture the entire tooth, from crown to root, providing detailed information on dental conditions. Occlusal X-rays capture the entire arch of teeth in one image, aiding in the evaluation of tooth development and spatial relationships within the jaw.</p></p>
