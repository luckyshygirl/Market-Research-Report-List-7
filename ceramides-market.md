<p><p>The ceramides market is categorized primarily into two types: fermentation ceramides and plant-extract ceramides. In contrast, plant-extract ceramides are sourced from natural plant materials, providing moisturizing and protective benefits, appealing to consumers seeking eco-friendly and sustainable options. Both types cater to the growing demand for effective and natural ingredients in cosmetics and personal care products.</p></p>
