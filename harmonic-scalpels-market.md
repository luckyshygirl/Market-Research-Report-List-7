<p><p>The Harmonic Scalpels market can be categorized into two main types: Attracting Harmonic Scalpels and Cutting Harmonic Scalpels. Attracting Harmonic Scalpels use ultrasonic technology to attract tissues, minimizing tissue damage and enhancing precision during surgeries. In contrast, Cutting Harmonic Scalpels utilize high-frequency oscillations to cut through tissue while simultaneously sealing blood vessels, reducing bleeding and promoting faster healing. Both types are widely used in surgical procedures, offering benefits such as decreased recovery time and improved surgical outcomes.</p></p>
