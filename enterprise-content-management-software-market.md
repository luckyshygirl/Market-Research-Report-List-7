<p><p>Enterprise Content Management (ECM) software is categorized into three main market types: cloud-based, on-premise, and Software as a Service (SaaS). Cloud-based ECM offers accessibility and scalability through online platforms, allowing users to manage content remotely. On-premise ECM involves installing software locally on company servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. SaaS ECM delivers applications via the internet on a subscription basis, enabling flexible access and automatic updates while minimizing upfront costs and maintenance burdens.</p></p>
