<p><strong>The Nuclear Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear imaging market consists of various types, including Positron Emission Tomography (PET), Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT), and Planar Scintigraphy Systems. PET utilizes radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes, offering high-resolution images for oncology and neurology. SPECT provides three-dimensional images by detecting gamma rays, commonly used in cardiac and brain imaging. Planar Scintigraphy captures two-dimensional images, primarily for bone scans and functional studies. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnostics, guiding treatment decisions, and monitoring disease progression.</p></p>
