<p><ul><li>Segmented Molds</li><li>Two-Piece Molds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire mold market includes segmented molds and two-piece molds. Segmented molds consist of multiple detachable sections, allowing for intricate tire designs and easier maintenance. They are ideal for producing complex tread patterns and large tires. Two-piece molds, simpler in design, are made of two halves that clamp together, suitable for standard tire production with straightforward tread designs. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, balancing complexity, cost, and efficiency in tire production processes.</p></p>
