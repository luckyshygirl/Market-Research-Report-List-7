<p><p>The Membrane Water Treatment Chemicals market encompasses various categories essential for enhancing water purification processes. Pretreatment chemicals, such as coagulants and flocculants, prepare raw water for filtration, removing suspended solids and reducing fouling. Biotreatment chemicals, including biocides and nutrients, facilitate the biological breakdown of contaminants, improving membrane performance. Sludge treatment chemicals target the handling and disposal of residual sludge, optimizing dewatering and minimizing environmental impact. Together, these chemicals ensure efficient and sustainable membrane-based water treatment systems.</p></p>
