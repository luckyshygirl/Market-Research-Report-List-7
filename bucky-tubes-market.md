<p><p>Bucky tubes, or carbon nanotubes, are categorized into two primary market types: single-wall and multi-wall buckytubes. Single-wall buckytubes consist of a single layer of carbon atoms rolled into a cylindrical shape, offering unique electrical and mechanical properties ideal for nanotechnology applications. In contrast, multi-wall buckytubes comprise multiple concentric layers of carbon, providing enhanced structural strength and stability. Both types have diverse applications in fields such as electronics, materials science, and energy, driving significant market interest and investment.</p></p>
