<p><ul><li>Continuous Type</li><li>Discontinuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared food dryer market comprises two primary types: continuous and discontinuous. Continuous infrared food dryers operate in a nonstop flow, allowing for uninterrupted processing of large batches, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, discontinuous infrared food dryers operate in batch modes, offering flexibility for smaller quantities or varied production schedules. Both types leverage infrared technology to efficiently remove moisture from food, preserving flavor, nutrients, and shelf life while catering to different production needs in the food industry.</p></p>
