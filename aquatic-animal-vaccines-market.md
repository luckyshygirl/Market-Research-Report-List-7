<p><p>The Aquatic Animal Vaccines Market primarily consists of two types: injectable vaccines and dip vaccines. Injectable vaccines are administered directly to individual fish, providing targeted immunization against specific diseases, which is crucial for high-value species. In contrast, dip vaccines involve immersing aquatic animals in a vaccine solution, allowing for mass immunization and easier application in larger populations. Both types aim to enhance disease resistance, improve animal health, and ensure sustainable aquaculture practices by reducing the reliance on antibiotics.</p></p>
