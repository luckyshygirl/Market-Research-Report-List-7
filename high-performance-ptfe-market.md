<p><p>The High Performance PTFE market encompasses various forms, including granular, aqueous dispersion, and micronized types. Granular PTFE is used for its excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications like seals and gaskets. Aqueous dispersion is utilized in coatings and additives, providing enhanced adhesion and performance in diverse industries. Micronized PTFE, with its fine particle size, is ideal for improving surface properties and reducing friction in applications like lubricants and plastics, enhancing overall product performance.</p></p>
