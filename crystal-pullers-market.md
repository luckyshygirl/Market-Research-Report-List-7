<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crystal pullers are essential equipment used in the growth of single crystals, crucial for applications in electronics, optics, and materials science. The Czochralski (CZ) method furnace enables the production of high-quality silicon and other crystalline materials by pulling a seed crystal from molten material. In contrast, the Floating Zone (FZ) method furnace utilizes a localized melting zone, allowing for the growth of high-purity crystals without contamination from crucibles. Both methods are vital in advancing semiconductor technology and the production of advanced materials.</p></p>
