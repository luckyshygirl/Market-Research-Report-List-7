<p><p>The functional protein market consists of various segments, each catering to specific nutritional needs. Whey protein isolates offer higher purity and lower lactose content, making them suitable for lactose-intolerant individuals. Casein is a slow-digesting protein, beneficial for sustained muscle support. Soy protein is a plant-based alternative, favored for its complete amino acid content and health benefits.</p></p>
