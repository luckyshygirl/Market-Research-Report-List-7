<p><p>The on-board electrical systems market is categorized into two main types: On-Board AC Electrical Systems and On-Board DC Electrical Systems. On-Board AC Electrical Systems utilize alternating current to power larger appliances and systems, commonly found in vehicles requiring higher power levels, such as buses and trains. In contrast, On-Board DC Electrical Systems use direct current, typically serving smaller devices like lights, sensors, and electronic controls in personal vehicles. Both systems are essential for efficient energy management in modern transportation.</p></p>
