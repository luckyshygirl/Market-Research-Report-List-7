<p><p>The fluid control equipment market encompasses various types that regulate and manage fluid movement. Valves control flow and pressure, ensuring proper distribution. Actuators are devices that enable valve operation, often driven by electrical or pneumatic signals. Instruments monitor fluid parameters such as temperature and pressure, providing critical data. Pneumatic components utilize air pressure for control, while hydraulic components use liquid pressure for power. Other market types may include filters, regulators, and flow meters, contributing to overall system efficiency and safety.</p></p>
