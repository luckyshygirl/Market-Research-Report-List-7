<p><p>The Ultra High Speed Machine Vision Cameras market encompasses two main types: Line Scan Cameras and Area Scan Cameras. Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed applications like inspection in manufacturing processes where continuous movement occurs. In contrast, Area Scan Cameras capture entire frames at once, suitable for static or slower-moving objects. Both types offer high-resolution imaging and high-speed performance, catering to diverse industrial automation and quality control needs.</p></p>
