<p><p>The Bioreactor Dissolved Oxygen (DO) Sensors market is categorized by sensor type into two main types: optical and polarographic sensors. Optical sensors utilize fluorescence principles to measure DO levels, offering advantages like lower maintenance and wider measurement range. Polarographic sensors rely on an electrochemical process, providing real-time readings but requiring regular calibration. Each sensor type caters to specific bioprocessing needs, impacting cell growth and product yield based on their responsiveness and accuracy in monitoring oxygen levels in bioreactor systems.</p></p>
