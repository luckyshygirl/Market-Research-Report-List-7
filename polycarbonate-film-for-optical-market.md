<p><ul><li>Optically Isotropic Film</li><li>Optically Anisotropic Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polycarbonate film for the optical market is divided into two main types: optically isotropic film and optically anisotropic film. Optically isotropic film exhibits uniform optical properties in all directions, making it ideal for applications requiring consistent light transmission and clarity. In contrast, optically anisotropic film has varying properties depending on the direction of light, often used for advanced applications like LCD displays and optical devices where specific light manipulation is needed. Both types serve distinct, critical roles in optical technologies.</p></p>
