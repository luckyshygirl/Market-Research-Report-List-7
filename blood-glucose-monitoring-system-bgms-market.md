<p><p>The Blood Glucose Monitoring System (BGMS) includes devices and consumables used to measure blood sugar levels, crucial for managing diabetes. Key types in this market are blood glucose test strips and blood glucose meters. Blood glucose test strips are disposable components that collect a blood sample and display glucose levels when inserted into a meter. Blood glucose meters are handheld devices that analyze the blood sample and provide real-time glucose readings, enabling effective diabetes management and lifestyle adjustments.</p></p>
