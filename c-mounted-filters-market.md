<p><p>C-mounted filters are optical devices used in various applications, such as imaging and sensing, to selectively transmit certain wavelengths of light while blocking others. Bandpass filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass through, making them suitable for applications like fluorescence microscopy. Longpass filters, on the other hand, permit wavelengths longer than a certain cut-off to pass, effectively blocking shorter wavelengths. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing image quality and ensuring accurate data collection in scientific and industrial settings.</p></p>
