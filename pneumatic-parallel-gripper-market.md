<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic parallel gripper market is segmented into single acting and double acting types. Single acting grippers utilize air pressure for movement in one direction, relying on a spring mechanism for the return movement, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, double acting grippers use air pressure for both opening and closing actions, providing greater precision and control, making them suitable for more complex applications. Both types cater to varied automation needs in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
