<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The In-Mold Labeled (IML) Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow IML Containers</li><li>Injection IML Containers</li><li>Thermoforming IML Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Mold Labeling (IML) containers are categorized into three main types based on their manufacturing processes. Blow IML containers are made using blow molding, ideal for lightweight designs and complex shapes. Injection IML containers utilize injection molding, offering high precision and durability, suitable for rigid packaging. Thermoforming IML containers are created through heating flat sheets into molds, allowing for versatile designs and lower production costs. These methods enhance branding and provide a seamless, attractive finish for various packaging applications.</p></p>
