<p><p>The rapid prototyping equipment market encompasses various technologies for additive manufacturing. Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA) uses UV light to cure resin layers, producing highly detailed models. Three Dimensional Printing (3DP) applies inkjet technology to build parts from powders. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) utilizes lasers to fuse powdered materials, ideal for functional prototypes. Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) involves stacking and bonding layers of material. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) melts thermoplastic filament to create prototypes layer by layer. Other methods include digital light processing and binder jetting, enhancing versatility.</p></p>
