<p><ul><li>TPE</li><li>PUR</li><li>EPDM</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto glass encapsulation market encompasses various thermoplastic and thermosetting materials used for bonding and sealing automotive glass. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomers) offers flexibility and durability, making it suitable for dynamic applications. PUR (Polyurethane) provides strong adhesion and excellent weather resistance. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is favored for its outstanding elasticity and ozone resistance, while PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is used for cost-effective solutions due to its rigidity and low production costs. Each material serves specific performance and application needs in vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>
