<p><p>The Hem Flange Structural Adhesive market consists of two primary types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use, requiring no mixing and typically curing upon exposure to moisture or heat. Conversely, two-component adhesives involve a mixing process, combining a resin and a hardener for enhanced bonding strength and durability. Both types are utilized in various applications, particularly in the automotive and manufacturing sectors, providing robust solutions for joining hem flanges in structural assemblies.</p></p>
