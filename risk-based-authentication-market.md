<p><p>The risk-based authentication market is divided into two main types: cloud authentication and on-premises authentication. Cloud authentication utilizes web-based services to assess user risk and validate identity, offering scalability and flexibility, often appealing to organizations seeking cost-effective solutions. On-premises authentication, in contrast, involves local servers and infrastructure, providing enhanced control and security for sensitive data. Both types aim to enhance user experience and security by dynamically adjusting authentication requirements based on the assessed risk level.</p></p>
