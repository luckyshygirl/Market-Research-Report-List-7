<p><p>MPEG (Moving Picture Experts Group) is a working group of experts that develops standards for audio and video compression and transmission. It is best known for creating widely used formats such as MPEG-1, MPEG-2, MPEG-4, and MPEG-7, which are integral to digital media, including streaming, broadcasting, and storage. These standards enable efficient compression, ensuring high-quality audio and video delivery across various platforms and devices.</p><p>The MPEG market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-quality video content, advancements in streaming technologies, and the proliferation of smart devices. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.8% during the forecast period. This growth is fueled by the rising adoption of over-the-top (OTT) platforms, 4K and 8K video resolutions, and the expansion of 5G networks, which enhance bandwidth and streaming capabilities.</p><p>Emerging trends in the MPEG market include the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) for improved video compression and real-time processing, as well as the development of immersive media formats like virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR). Additionally, the demand for efficient video codecs, such as H.265 (HEVC) and AV1, is growing to support higher resolutions and reduce data consumption. These trends are shaping the future of the MPEG market, making it a key player in the digital media landscape.</p></p>
