<p><p>The Servo Gearbox market includes various types designed for specific applications. Inline gearboxes have input and output shafts aligned in a straight line, offering compact and efficient power transmission. Right-angle gearboxes feature perpendicular input and output shafts, ideal for space-constrained setups requiring a 90-degree turn. Other types include planetary, harmonic, and custom configurations, catering to diverse industrial needs. Each type is optimized for precision, torque, and speed control, enhancing performance in automation, robotics, and machinery applications.</p></p>
