<p><p>The aircraft baggage scanners market primarily consists of two main types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners utilize high-energy radiation to produce images of baggage contents, allowing security personnel to identify potential threats. Millimeter wave scanners, on the other hand, use radio waves to detect concealed objects, offering a non-invasive inspection method that enhances passenger privacy. Both technologies are essential for airport security, ensuring the safe screening of luggage while maintaining efficient passenger flow.</p></p>
