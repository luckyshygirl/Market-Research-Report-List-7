<p><p>The Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) devices market comprises various types, including coronary stents, PTCA catheters, coronary guidewires, and embolic protection devices. Coronary stents are used to keep arteries open after balloon angioplasty, while PTCA catheters facilitate the delivery of therapeutic interventions. Coronary guidewires aid in navigating through the vascular system during procedures, ensuring accurate placement of devices. Embolic protection devices help prevent debris from traveling into the bloodstream during interventions, enhancing patient safety and procedural efficacy.</p></p>
