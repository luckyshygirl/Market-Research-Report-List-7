<p><p>The offshore drilling market is categorized into three main types: jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile platforms with legs that lift them above water, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are floating vessels anchored to the seabed, suitable for deeper waters and harsh conditions. Drillships are advanced ships equipped with drilling equipment, capable of operating in ultra-deep waters, offering high mobility and versatility. Each type serves specific operational needs in offshore exploration and production.</p></p>
