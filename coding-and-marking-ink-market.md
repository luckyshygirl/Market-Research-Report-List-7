<p><p>The coding and marking ink market comprises three primary types: water-based ink, oil-based ink, and hot melt ink. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, offering quick-drying and low VOC emissions, making them ideal for food packaging. Oil-based inks provide excellent durability and are resistant to smudging, often used in industrial applications. Hot melt inks, known for their adhesive properties, are utilized in labeling and packaging, allowing for efficient printing on various substrates while ensuring strong adhesion.</p></p>
