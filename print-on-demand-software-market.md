<p><p>Print On Demand (POD) software can be categorized into two main market types: integrated and independent. Integrated POD software is embedded within e-commerce platforms, allowing users to seamlessly manage printing and fulfillment alongside sales. This type often benefits from built-in tools and support, enhancing user convenience. In contrast, independent POD software functions as standalone solutions, offering greater customization and flexibility. Users of independent platforms may need to manage more components separately, but they often enjoy broader creative control over their products.</p></p>
