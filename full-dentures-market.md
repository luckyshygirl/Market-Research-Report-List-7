<p><p>Full dentures are removable dental appliances designed to replace an entire arch of missing teeth, restoring functionality and aesthetics for individuals who have lost all or most of their teeth due to various factors such as decay, gum disease, or injury. They are custom-made to fit the gums and provide support to the facial structure.</p><p>The Full Dentures Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by factors such as an aging population, increasing dental awareness, and improved dental restoration technologies. As more people seek solutions for tooth loss, the demand for full dentures continues to rise. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.10% during the forecast period, fueled by advancements in materials and techniques that enhance comfort and longevity. Furthermore, the rise in cosmetic dentistry and the societal emphasis on good oral health contribute to the growing adoption of full dentures. </p><p>Emerging trends include the integration of 3D printing technology in denture fabrication, which allows for precise customization and quicker production times. Additionally, the introduction of innovative materials that mimic the appearance of natural teeth is enhancing patient acceptance and satisfaction, further supporting market expansion.</p></p>
