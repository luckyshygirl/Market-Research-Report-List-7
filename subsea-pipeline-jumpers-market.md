<p><p>The subsea pipeline jumpers market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible pipeline jumpers. Rigid pipeline jumpers are typically made from materials like steel and are designed for specific alignment and configuration, providing stability in high-pressure environments. In contrast, flexible pipeline jumpers are constructed using materials like flexible composites or thermoplastic, allowing for greater adaptability and movement. Both types are essential for connecting subsea pipelines, ensuring efficient flow of fluids while accommodating seabed conditions and installation requirements.</p></p>
