<p><strong>The PEOs (Professional Employer Organizations) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Professional Employer Organizations (PEOs) are companies that provide comprehensive HR solutions, allowing businesses to outsource employee management tasks. The Full Service PEO model offers a co-employment relationship, managing payroll, benefits, and compliance while sharing employer responsibilities. In contrast, the Administrative Services Organization (ASO) market provides administrative support without co-employment. ASOs typically handle payroll and HR tasks while the client company retains responsibility for employee relations and compliance, allowing businesses greater control over their workforce management.</p></p>
