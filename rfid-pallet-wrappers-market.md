<p><strong>The RFID Pallet Wrappers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li><li>High Frequency and Near Field Communication</li><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID pallet wrappers are categorized based on frequency types that define their communication range and application. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) systems offer longer read ranges and faster data transfer, ideal for high-volume environments. High-Frequency (HF) systems, commonly used in short-range applications, provide moderate read distances with reliable performance around obstacles. Low-Frequency (LF) options are suitable for tracking items over shorter distances but with enhanced penetration capabilities. Dual Frequency systems combine features of both HF and LF, enabling versatile applications across various industries.</p></p>
