<p><p>The lifting thread devices market is categorized into resorbable and permanent types. Resorbable lifting threads are designed to dissolve within the body over time, providing temporary lifting and support while promoting natural tissue regeneration. In contrast, permanent lifting threads remain in place indefinitely, offering lasting support and enhancement. Both types cater to aesthetic and medical needs, with resorbable options favored for their lower long-term commitment and reduced risk of complications, while permanent threads provide sustained results for more profound effects.</p></p>
