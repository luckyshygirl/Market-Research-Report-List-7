<p><p>Textile waste management comprises various market types aimed at minimizing environmental impact. Source reduction solutions focus on preventing textile waste at its origin through sustainable design and production practices. Landfill solutions address the safe disposal of textiles but are less environmentally friendly. Recycling solutions involve converting waste textiles into new products, fostering a circular economy. Incineration solutions include high-temperature combustion of waste textiles, generating energy but raising concerns about emissions and environmental health. Together, these solutions aim to alleviate textile waste challenges.</p></p>
