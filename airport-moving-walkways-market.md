<p><p>The airport moving walkways market is categorized into two main types: belt type and pallet type. Belt type walkways utilize a looped belt system to transport passengers, offering a smooth and efficient ride. In contrast, pallet type walkways, which feature flat, stationary surfaces supported by a moving mechanism, provide a more stable platform for users. Both types enhance passenger flow and reduce walking distances, contributing to improved airport efficiency and passenger convenience in busy terminals.</p></p>
