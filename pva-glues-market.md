<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVA (Polyvinyl Acetate) glues are categorized into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I PVA glues are water-resistant, ideal for outdoor applications or projects exposed to moisture. They provide stronger adhesion in humid conditions. Type II PVA glues, on the other hand, are not as water-resistant and are better suited for indoor use, such as crafts and light woodworking. Both types are favored for their ease of use, fast drying time, and non-toxic properties.</p></p>
