<p><strong>The Snow Melting Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Type</li><li>Constant Wattage Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow melting cable market consists of two primary types: self-regulating and constant wattage cables. Self-regulating cables automatically adjust their heat output based on ambient temperature changes, providing efficient energy use and preventing overheating. In contrast, constant wattage cables deliver a fixed amount of heat per unit length, offering predictability in performance but potentially higher energy consumption. Both types are essential for maintaining safety and accessibility in snow-prone areas by effectively melting snow and ice on driveways, walkways, and roofs.</p></p>
