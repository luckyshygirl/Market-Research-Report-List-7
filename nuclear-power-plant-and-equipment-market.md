<p><p>The nuclear power plant equipment market consists of various reactor types, including Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR), which use water at high pressure to prevent boiling; Boiling Water Reactors (BWR), where water directly boils in the reactor core; and Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR), utilizing heavy water as a coolant and moderator. High Temperature Gas-Cooled Reactors (HTGR) utilize gas as a coolant for enhanced thermal efficiency. Other reactor types include advanced and experimental designs, catering to diverse energy needs and safety standards.</p></p>
