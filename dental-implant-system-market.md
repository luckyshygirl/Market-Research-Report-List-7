<p><p>The dental implant system market consists of three primary components: implants, abutments, and crowns. Implants are titanium posts inserted into the jawbone, serving as artificial roots for teeth. Abutments are connectors that attach to implants, providing a framework for the crown. Crowns are custom-made prosthetic teeth placed on the abutment, restoring function and aesthetics. Together, these elements create a comprehensive solution for tooth replacement, catering to various patient needs and preferences in dental care.</p></p>
