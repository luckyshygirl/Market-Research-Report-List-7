<p><p>Liquid glucose, also known as glucose syrup, is a thick, clear, and sweet solution derived from the hydrolysis of starch, typically from corn, wheat, or potatoes. It is widely used in the food and beverage industry as a sweetener, thickener, and humectant, and is a key ingredient in products like confectionery, baked goods, dairy items, and beverages. Its functional properties, such as preventing crystallization in candies and enhancing texture, make it a versatile ingredient.</p><p>The Liquid Glucose Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
