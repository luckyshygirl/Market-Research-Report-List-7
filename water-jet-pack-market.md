<p><p>The water jet pack market features two primary types based on propulsion methods: hydrogen peroxide-driven and fuel-driven systems. Hydrogen peroxide-driven units utilize a chemical reaction to produce steam, which powers the jet propulsion, offering a cleaner energy option with minimal emissions. In contrast, fuel-driven jet packs rely on combustion engines that use traditional fuels, providing robust performance but at the expense of higher emissions. Both types appeal to adventure enthusiasts, offering unique experiences in water jet propulsion technology.</p></p>
