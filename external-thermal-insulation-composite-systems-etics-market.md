<p><p>External Thermal Insulation Composite Systems (ETICS) are construction methods that provide thermal insulation to building exteriors, improving energy efficiency and reducing heat loss. The integration of smart technology into ETICS and a shift towards eco-friendly materials are also notable trends. Additionally, regions with extreme climates are increasingly opting for ETICS as a solution for maintaining indoor comfort, further fueling market expansion.
