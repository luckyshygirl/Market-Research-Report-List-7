<p><p>Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) Clarifiers are advanced water treatment systems used to separate suspended solids, oils, and greases from water. Utilizing a process where air is dissolved under pressure and then released into the wastewater stream, DAF systems facilitate the formation of microbubbles that attach to the contaminants, causing them to rise to the surface for easy removal. This technology is particularly beneficial in industrial applications, municipal wastewater treatment, and in the food and beverage industry.</p><p>The DAF Clarifiers Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.
