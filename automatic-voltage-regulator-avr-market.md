<p><p>An Automatic Voltage Regulator (AVR) ensures stable voltage output, crucial for protecting electrical equipment. The market types include Linear Regulators, which provide smooth output by dissipating excess voltage as heat, ideal for low-noise applications. Shunt Voltage Regulators maintain voltage by diverting excess current, suitable for applications with limited load variations. Other market types may include switching regulators, which convert input voltage efficiently and are used in high-power devices. Together, these regulators facilitate reliable power supply across various sectors.</p></p>
