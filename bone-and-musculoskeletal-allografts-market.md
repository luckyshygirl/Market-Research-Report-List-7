<p><p>The Bone and Musculoskeletal Allografts market comprises various types, including Corticocancellous allografts, Massive Structural allografts, and Osteoinductive allografts. Corticocancellous allografts combine cortical and cancellous bone, offering both structural support and osteoconductive properties, making them ideal for orthopedic surgeries. Massive Structural allografts are used for reconstructing large bone defects, providing substantial strength and stability. Osteoinductive allografts contain growth factors that promote the formation of new bone, facilitating healing and regeneration in complex musculoskeletal conditions.</p></p>
