<p><strong>The TIRF Imaging Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prism Type</li><li>Objective Len Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TIRF (Total Internal Reflection Fluorescence) imaging microscopes utilize different configurations to achieve high-resolution imaging. The Prism Type uses a prism to create total internal reflection, exciting fluorophores near the glass-water interface, resulting in a thin fluorescent layer for high-contrast images. In contrast, the Objective Lens Type integrates the TIRF mechanism within the objective lens itself, providing a more compact design and easier sample positioning. Both types are crucial for studying cellular processes with enhanced depth resolution and minimal background noise.</p></p>
