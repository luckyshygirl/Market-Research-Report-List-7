<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LAN Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switching</li><li>Layer 3 Switching</li><li>Layer 4 Switching</li><li>Multi-Layer switching (MLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LAN switch market is categorized into various types based on their functionality. Layer 2 switches operate at the data link layer, providing MAC address-based forwarding. Layer 3 switches integrate routing capabilities and operate at the network layer, enabling IP-based forwarding. Layer 4 switches offer traffic management by analyzing transport layer protocols, enhancing performance and QoS. Multi-Layer Switching (MLS) combines features of both Layer 2 and Layer 3 switches, allowing for efficient handling of data across multiple layers of the OSI model.</p></p>
