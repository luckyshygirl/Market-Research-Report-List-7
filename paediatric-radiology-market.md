<p><p>The pediatric radiology market encompasses various imaging modalities crucial for diagnosing conditions in children. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves for safe imaging, particularly for soft tissues. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed images of organs and tissues without radiation, making it suitable for younger patients. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-ray images to create cross-sectional views, effective for complex diagnoses but involves radiation exposure. X-ray remains a widely used technique for quick assessments of bone and lung conditions. Other methods may include fluoroscopy or nuclear medicine.</p></p>
