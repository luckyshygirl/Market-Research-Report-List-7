<p><p>The digital walkie-talkie market is primarily divided into two types: VHF (Very High Frequency) and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). VHF walkie-talkies operate in the frequency range of 30 to 300 MHz, offering longer range and better performance in open areas, making them suitable for outdoor activities and maritime communication. Conversely, UHF walkie-talkies function within 300 to 3,000 MHz, providing superior penetration through buildings and obstacles, ideal for urban environments and industrial settings. Both types cater to diverse communication needs.</p></p>
