<p><strong>The Cable Granulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type Copper Cable Granulator</li><li>Wet Type Copper Cable Granulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable granulator market consists of two primary types: dry type and wet type copper cable granulators. Dry type granulators utilize mechanical processes to effectively separate copper from insulation without using water, making them energy-efficient and easier to operate. In contrast, wet type granulators employ a water-based system that enhances the separation process, allowing for higher purity of recovered copper but typically requiring more maintenance and operational costs. Both types cater to different operational needs in the recycling industry.</p></p>
