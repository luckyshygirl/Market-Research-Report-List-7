<p><ul><li>Extrusion Grade</li><li>Injection molding Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MS Polymer market encompasses two main types: extrusion grade and injection molding grade. Extrusion grade is primarily used for producing continuous profiles, sheets, and films through processes like thermoplastic extrusion, focusing on flowability and surface finish. Injection molding grade, on the other hand, is tailored for creating complex shapes and intricate parts, emphasizing dimensional accuracy and mechanical strength. Both grades cater to various applications across industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods, where durability and flexibility are essential.</p></p>
