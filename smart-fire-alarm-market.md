<p><strong>The Smart Fire Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart fire alarm market includes three main types: photoelectric, dual sensors, and ionization alarms. Photoelectric alarms detect smoldering fires using light beams, making them effective for early smoke detection. Dual sensor alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies, offering a wider range of fire detection. Ionization alarms are sensitive to fast-burning fires, responding quickly to flames. Each type serves distinct environments and preferences, enhancing safety through advanced technology and timely alerts in case of fire hazards.</p></p>
