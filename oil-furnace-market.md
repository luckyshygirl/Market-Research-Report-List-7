<p><ul><li>Condensing Oil-Fired Furnace</li><li>Oil Fired Indirect Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil furnace market primarily includes condensing oil-fired furnaces and oil-fired indirect heaters. Condensing oil-fired furnaces are designed to maximize energy efficiency by utilizing exhaust gases, leading to lower fuel consumption and reduced emissions. In contrast, oil-fired indirect heaters provide heat by transferring warmth from oil to air or water, using a heating element without direct combustion. Both types cater to residential and commercial heating needs, offering various efficiency levels and operational costs to suit different user requirements.</p></p>
