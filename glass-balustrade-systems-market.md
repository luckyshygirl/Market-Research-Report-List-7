<p><p>The glass balustrade systems market comprises various types, primarily structural glass balustrades and frameless glass balustrades. Structural glass balustrades utilize polished glass panels secured with metal supports, offering stability while maintaining visibility. In contrast, frameless glass balustrades feature glass units that are fixed directly to the floor or walls without visible supports, providing a sleek, uninterrupted view. Both types enhance safety and aesthetics in commercial and residential spaces, making them popular choices for modern architectural designs.</p></p>
