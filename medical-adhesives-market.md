<p><p>The medical adhesives market consists of various types: Water Type adhesives are typically aqueous solutions that offer low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them suitable for sensitive applications. Solvent Type adhesives contain organic solvents and provide strong bonding, but may pose health risks due to volatile compounds. Solids refer to adhesives that are in solid form, offering durability and high strength, while Hot Melt Type adhesives are thermoplastic materials that become pliable upon heating, allowing for fast and effective bonding in medical devices.</p></p>
