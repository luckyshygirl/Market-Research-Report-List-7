<p><p>The car ferry market includes monohull and multihull designs. Monohull ferries, with a single hull, are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used for short to medium routes. They offer stability and efficiency in calmer waters. Multihull ferries, featuring two or more hulls, excel in speed, fuel efficiency, and smoother rides in rough seas, making them ideal for longer routes. While monohulls dominate due to lower construction costs, multihulls are gaining popularity for their advanced performance and environmental benefits, catering to evolving passenger and operational demands.</p></p>
