Additionally, the shift toward electric and hybrid vehicles with AWD capabilities is contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Key trends in the AWD market include the integration of advanced electronic control systems for better power distribution, the development of lightweight AWD systems to improve fuel efficiency, and the rising adoption of AWD in electric vehicles (EVs) to optimize performance.
