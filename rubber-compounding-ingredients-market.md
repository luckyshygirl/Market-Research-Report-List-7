<p><p>The rubber compounding ingredients market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Vulcanizing Agents** enhance rubber's heat resistance and elasticity.</p><p>2. **Protective Agents** safeguard against environmental factors, extending product life.</p><p>3. **Reinforcing Agents** improve strength and durability, often including carbon black or silica.</p><p>4. **Bonding Agents** ensure adhesion between rubber and other materials, crucial for composite products.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass a range of additives affecting properties like color, flexibility, and processing. Each ingredient plays a vital role in optimizing rubber performance for various applications.</p></p>
