<p><p>The Low Conductivity Coolant market includes Single Phase and Dual Phase Coolants. Single Phase Coolants remain in liquid form throughout the cooling process, offering consistent thermal management and simplicity in design. They are ideal for applications requiring stable heat transfer. Dual Phase Coolants, on the other hand, transition between liquid and gas phases, providing enhanced cooling efficiency through latent heat absorption. They are suited for high-performance systems where rapid heat dissipation is critical. Both types cater to industries like electronics, automotive, and energy, ensuring optimal thermal regulation.</p></p>
