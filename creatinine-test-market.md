<p><ul><li>Blood Test</li><li>Urine Test and Creatinine Clearance Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The creatinine test market includes three primary types: blood tests, urine tests, and creatinine clearance tests. The blood test measures serum creatinine levels, indicating kidney function. Urine tests assess the concentration of creatinine in urine over a specific period, helping evaluate kidney health. Creatinine clearance tests combine blood and urine measurements to determine the kidneys' efficiency in filtering creatinine. Each type serves as an essential tool for diagnosing and monitoring kidney-related conditions, influencing patient management and treatment decisions.</p></p>
