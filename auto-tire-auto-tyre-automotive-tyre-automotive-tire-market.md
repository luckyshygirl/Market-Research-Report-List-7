<p><p>The automotive tire market primarily consists of two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires, constructed with crisscrossed layers of rubber-coated fabric plies, offer durability and load-bearing capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles and off-road applications. Radial tires, featuring perpendicular ply cords and a steel belt, provide better fuel efficiency, traction, and comfort, making them ideal for passenger cars and high-speed vehicles. The radial tire market dominates due to its superior performance, while bias tires remain relevant in specific industrial and off-road sectors.</p></p>
