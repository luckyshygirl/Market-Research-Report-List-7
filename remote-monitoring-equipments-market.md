<p><p>The remote monitoring equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: external and implantable remote monitoring equipment. External remote monitoring equipment includes devices worn on the body, such as smartwatches and fitness trackers, which collect health data remotely, aiding in real-time monitoring. In contrast, implantable remote monitoring equipment consists of devices surgically placed within the body, like cardiac monitors, which continuously track vital health metrics. Both types enhance patient care by enabling continuous health assessments outside clinical settings.</p></p>
