<p><p>Wheel fatigue testing equipment evaluates the durability and performance of wheels under various stress conditions. **Radial Fatigue Testing Equipment** simulates vertical loads to assess how wheels withstand forces from road surfaces over time. **Cornering Fatigue Testing Equipment** replicates lateral forces during turning, testing wheel strength under side loads. **Other types** include multi-axis or combined load testing, which evaluates wheels under complex, real-world conditions like braking or impact. These systems ensure wheels meet safety and performance standards, catering to automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
